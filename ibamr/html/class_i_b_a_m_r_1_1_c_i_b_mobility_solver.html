Detailed Description

Class CIBMobilitySolver solves for the constraint forces and rigid body velocity of the structure(s). Specifically, the class solves two types of matrix-equations.

Here, is the interpolation operator, is the spreading operator, is the incompressible Stokes operator, is the rigid body operator, is the desired velocity at the nodes of the structure(s), and is the net external force and torque on the body.

This class employs direct solver for the approximate mobility and body-mobility sub-problems. The approximate mobility matrix is intended to be used in the preconditioning step of the overall constraint solver. The overall preconditioner is implemented in
